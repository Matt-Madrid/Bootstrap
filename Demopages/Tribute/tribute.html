RUSS

Russell James Vitale, better known by his stage name Russ is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer and author from Atlanta, Georgia. He is best known for his singles "What They Want", "Losin Control" and "Best on Earth",which peaked respectively at number 83, 63 and 46 on the US Billboard Hot 100. “Do whatever you like, go wherever you want, love however you feel it to be true. For if you wait, life won't do the same” -RUSS
